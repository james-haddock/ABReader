Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How do I upload my own ebooks?
To upload your own ebooks, simply click on the 'Upload' button in the interface and select the ebook file from your device.
Can I upload audiobooks as well?
Yes, you can upload audiobooks in addition to ebooks. Just follow the same process of clicking on the 'Upload' button and selecting the audiobook file.
Is there a limit to the number of ebooks and audiobooks I can upload?
No, there is no limit to the number of ebooks and audiobooks you can upload. You can upload as many as you want and browse through your entire collection in the library.
How can I sync between listening to an audiobook and reading the ebook?
To seamlessly sync between listening to an audiobook and reading the ebook, simply click on the 'Sync' button in the interface. This will ensure that your progress is synchronized across both formats.
Can I access my library from different devices?
Yes, you can access your library from different devices as long as you are logged in with your account. Your library and progress will be synced across all devices.
